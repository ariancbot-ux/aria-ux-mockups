2-Way Drop-Chase Architecture

PHASE 1: Lead Entry → Propensity Score

Customer enquires about XUV700 → Netcore captures intent + preference (WhatsApp/Email) → AI scores conversion likelihood

PHASE 2: Channel-First Engagement

WhatsApp: "Hi Arjun, interested in the XUV700? What's your timeline?" (2-way conversation starts)

PHASE 3: Intelligent Drop-Chase

No response in 24h → Switch to Email (spec sheet) → Still no click → SMS reminder → If needed, phone handoff to dealer

PHASE 4: Closed-Loop Booking

Test drive booked → Reminder SMS 24h before → Post-drive re-nurture if not converted
